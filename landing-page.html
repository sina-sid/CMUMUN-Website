Fees

For schools interested in registering, we have decided on our school and delegate fee structure for this year. There will be three registration windows: early registration, regular registration, and late registration. Early registration will give all delegates a $5 discount, whereas late registration will add a $5 fee.

Registration Period School Fee Delegate Fee Deadline Refund Policy
Early $55 per delegation $45 per delegate by November 30, 2016 75% of cost
Regular $60 per delegation $50 per delegate by January 31, 2017 50% of cost
Late $65 per delegation $55 per delegate by February 21, 2017 0% of cost

To register, please follow this link and complete the conference registration process for your school. If you are unsure of your delegation's exact size, please give your best estimate; all registrations can be modified up to the registration deadline.
